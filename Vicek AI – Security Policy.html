Security Policy

Vicek AI maintains administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect user information.

Security Measures

  • Encrypted data transmission (TLS/HTTPS)
  • Cloud-based secure infrastructure
  • Access controls and authentication safeguards
  • Regular monitoring for vulnerabilities

Incident Response

In the event of a material security incident, affected users will be notified as required by applicable law.

Responsible Disclosure

Security vulnerabilities may be reported to: support@vicekai.com
